Information & Directions


The Church:

St. Martin in the Fields is located in a residential neighborhood and is bordered by Glenlake Ave, Keele St. and Indian Grove Rd. There is free street parking on the residential streets surrounding the church. There is also a TTC parking lot at the bottom of Indian Grove Rd., near Bloor St [the Keele Subway stop] which is free on weekends and a quick 4 minute walk Northwards to the church. The lot can only be accessed from Indian Rd., which runs north from Bloor St. and is parallel to Indian Grove Rd. [see map provided]. There is however a convenient paved foot path from the parking lot to Indian Grove Rd.


From the 401 East Bound to the Church

Take the 401 E.B to 427 south. Merge Left and continue on the Gardiner expressway E.B . Exit at Lakeshore and continue E.B until you reach Parkside Drive. Turn left on to Parkside and continue north [Parkside becomes Keele as it passes Bloor St.] until you reach Glenlake Ave., which is one block north of Bloor St. The church will be on your right. To reach the TTC parking lot, turn right on to Bloor st. and then turn left on to Indian Rd. The parking lot will be within a few metres, on your left.


From 401 West Bound to the Church


Take the 401 W.B to the DVP S.B. Merge left on to the Gardiner Expressway W.B and continue until you reach the Jameson/Lakeshore Blvd. exit. Follow the signs to Lakeshore and continue W.B until you reach Parkside Rd. Turn right onto Parkside Rd and follow directions as indicated above.


From the Church to the Venue { Atlantis Pavilion }:


Proceed South bound on Keele St, which will eventually turn into Parkside Rd., until you reach Lakeshore BLVD. Once at Lakeshore Blvd turn left / East Bound and proceed on Lakeshore Blvd as our venue, Atlantis Pavilion, will be on your right hand side and enter via Remembrance Drive.


The Venue { Atlantis Pavilion }:

Complimentary parking has been arranged for your convenience. As there may be other events at the venue, please obtain a pass from parking attendants by referencing the Edward/Gallan Wedding BEFORE parking your car. Golf carts will be available to escort you to the venues front entrance from the parking lot. Cars can be left overnight at no extra cost. There will be lots to eat and drink, so please plan ahead and plan to have a wonderful, worry free celebration with us.
