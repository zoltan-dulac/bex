Jody and Rebecca

August 14, 2014

Jody

"I fell in love with Jody at first sight", Rebecca told me when she first told me about her new beau a year ago. "Not only is he smart, handsome and funny, but he is also a gentleman and will carry me up three stories to bed when I have passed out after a Friday night of debauchery with Ele. If that is not true love, I have no idea what is!"

Rebecca

"I have never met anyone quite like Rebecca before", Jody confessed to me while driving me to my rehab appointment one Saturday afternoon. "She told me on our first date that I was kinda cute for a white guy, and that my Québecois accent made her weak in the knees. I actually thought about putting a restraining order on her, but, well, she is a Crown Attorney, so I guess I'm stuck with her for now."
