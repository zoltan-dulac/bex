Jody and Rebecca

October 12, 2014

Jody

After years of searching for the right guy but refusing to compromise or settle, I‘d all but given up hope on meeting “my guy”. Thankfully some very pushy and persuasive family and friends convinced me to give internet dating a try. I’m obviously now very thankful to them for this, or I would never have met my wonderful, handsome, thoughtful, witty, family oriented, Habs loving, Irish-French Canadian from Mississauga. I feel like I’ve known Jods all my life and that my heart has truly found a home...finally! =) While we are very different in many ways, we are similar where it counts and our differences make things interesting ;-) I’m so excited & happy to be marrying “my guy”, the amazing Jody Joseph Russell and can’t wait to start our lives together.

Rebecca

It's not every day you come across someone as wonderful as Rebecca. We have been together for over a year now. I knew from the moment I met Rebecca there was something special between us. Even though it was a slow dating process ;-) it grew into a wonderful relationship which lead me to known that Rebecca would be the one I asked to marry me. I have never cared so much for anyone as I do for Rebecca. Oct 12, 2014 will be another way for me to express how much I care for her and can’t wait to continue our life’s adventure as One. Watch out world as here comes team RODY!!!

    Church

    St. Martin in the Fields is located in a residential neighborhood and is bordered by Glenlake Ave, Keele St. and Indian Grove Rd. There is free street parking on the residential streets surrounding the church. There is also a TTC parking lot at the bottom of Indian Grove Rd., near Bloor St [the Keele Subway stop] which is free on weekends and a quick 4 minute walk Northwards to the church. The lot can only be accessed from Indian Rd., which runs north from Bloor St. and is parallel to Indian Grove Rd. [see map provided]. There is however a convenient paved foot path from the parking lot to Indian Grove Rd.
